《Big English》第二版第六册（Student's Book 6）**的核心词汇与重点句型。第六册作为该系列的进阶大纲，涵盖了从校园生活到全球未解之谜、未来科技及历史遗产等深层话题。
以下是按单元汇总的核心内容：
入门单元：Welcome to Class! (校园生活)
• 核心词汇： advice (建议), assignment (作业/任务), field trip (实地考察), schedule (课程表/时间表), essay (短文/论文)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 经历询问： "Have you ever been late for school?"。
    ◦ 给予建议： "You should always listen to the teacher." / "You shouldn’t leave your homework until the last minute."。
第1单元：All About School (校园活动与借口)
• 核心活动词汇： do a book report (做读书报告), finish a project (完成项目), hand in an assignment (提交作业), study for a test (为考试复习)。
• 重点句型（现在完成时）：
    ◦ 使用 yet, already, ever： "Has she done her homework yet?" / "She has already done it."。
    ◦ 寻找借口： "There was a power cut, and I couldn’t find my flashlight."。
第2单元：Amazing Young People (成就与目标)
• 核心成就词汇： become a doctor (成为医生), invent something (发明某物), start a company (开办公司), win a tournament (赢得比赛), publish a book (出版书)。
• 重点句型（完成时态对比）：
    ◦ 现在完成时（持续状态）： "How long have you lived here? I’ve lived here for five years."。
    ◦ 现在完成进行时（持续动作）： "How long has she been singing? She’s been singing since she was four."。
第3单元：Dilemmas (道德困境与价值观)
• 核心伦理词汇： ethical (伦理的/道德的), perspective (观点/视角), respectful (尊重的), treat (对待), dilemma (困境), feel guilty (感到内疚), get into trouble (陷入麻烦)。
• 重点句型（第一条件句）：
    ◦ "If I keep the wallet, I’ll feel guilty."。
    ◦ "If I tell my friend I lost her necklace, she’ll be upset with me."。
第4单元：Future Me (未来的预测)
• 核心未来词汇： earn a good salary (挣高薪), raise a family (养家), adventurous vacations (探险度假), nanotechnology (纳米技术), revolutionize (变革)。
• 重点句型（将来进行时）：
    ◦ 使用 definitely 和 probably： "In fifty years, I’ll probably be living in France." / "I definitely won’t be living with my parents."。
第5单元：If I Could Fly... (超能力与假设)
• 核心超能力词汇： become invisible (变隐身), fly (飞), have superhuman strength (拥有神力), read people’s minds (读心术), travel through time (时空旅行)。
• 重点句型（第二条件句/虚拟语气）：
    ◦ "If you could fly, where would you go?" / "If I could fly, I’d go to the moon."。
    ◦ 给予虚拟建议： "If I were you, I’d choose something else."。
第6单元：The Coolest School Subjects (学科与事实对比)
• 核心学科词汇： literature (文学), biology (生物), social studies (社会研究), democracy (民主), prime numbers (质数), playwrights (剧作家)。
• 重点句型（数量比较）：
    ◦ 比较级： "My teacher gives more homework than your teacher." / "I take fewer school subjects than my brother."。
    ◦ 最高级： "Jenny has the fewest cards." / "She has the least space."。
第7单元：Mysteries! (神秘现象)
• 核心神秘词汇： Atlantis (亚特兰蒂斯), Northern Lights (北极光), Bermuda Triangle (百慕大三角), crop circles (麦田怪圈), phenomenon (现象), proof (证据), theory (理论)。
• 重点句型（反义疑问句）：
    ◦ "The geoglyphs are in Peru, aren’t they?"。
    ◦ "Scientists can’t explain crop circles, can they?"。
第8单元：Why Is It Famous? (名胜古迹)
• 核心建筑词汇： cathedral (大教堂), mausoleum (陵墓), monument (纪念碑), palace (宫殿), pyramid (金字塔), statue (雕像), temple (寺庙)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 被动语态： "Stonehenge was constructed more than 4,000 years ago."。
    ◦ 定语从句（who/that）： "Louis XIV was a French king who ruled for 72 years." / "The Statue of Liberty is a landmark that has become a symbol of welcome."。
第9单元：That’s Entertainment! (娱乐与转述)
• 核心娱乐词汇： book signing (签名会), comic book exhibit (漫画展), festival (节庆), movie premiere (电影首映), sequel (续集)。
• 重点句型（间接引语）：
    ◦ "Sara said (that) the sequel wasn’t as good as the first movie."。
    ◦ "Paul said (that) he was going to the concert."。
