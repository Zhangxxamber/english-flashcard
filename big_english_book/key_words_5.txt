《Big English》第二版第五册（Student's Book 5）**的核心词汇与重点句型。这一册的内容大幅增加了抽象词汇、社会生活话题（如筹款、职业生涯、未来科技）以及更复杂的语法结构。
以下是各单元的核心汇总：
入门单元：Welcome to Class!
• 核心词汇：
    ◦ 活动： play baseball/soccer/basketball (打球类运动), play the guitar/piano (弹奏吉他/钢琴)。
    ◦ 运动与爱好： acting (表演), chess club (象棋俱乐部), drama club (戏剧俱乐部)。
    ◦ 形容词与时间： difficult (困难的), interesting (有趣的), good at (擅长), tonight (今晚), this morning (今天早上)。
• 重点句型： 复习过去式（She played tennis yesterday. / They didn’t eat pasta.）。
第1单元：My Interests (个人兴趣与社团)
• 核心活动词汇： build robots (造机器人), do martial arts (做武术), paint (绘画), play the trumpet (吹小号), read comics (读漫画), take pictures (拍照), write articles (写文章)。
• 校内团队： art club (艺术俱乐部), school newspaper (校报), school orchestra (学校管弦乐队), science club (科学俱乐部), tae kwon do club (跆拳道社), track team (田径队)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 建议： "How about joining the baseball team?"。
    ◦ 语法点： 在How about, love, like, enjoy, be interested in, be good at之后使用动名词（V-ing）。
第2单元：Family Ties (家庭纽带与人生大事)
• 人生大事 (Life events)： be born (出生), find/get a job (找工作), get married (结婚), graduate (毕业), move (搬家), retire (退休)。
• 家庭成员： aunt (婶婶/姨妈), uncle (叔叔/舅舅), baby sister (小妹妹), older brother (哥哥)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 过去时间： "We moved to Miami when I was ten." / "She visited Mexico City three years ago."。
    ◦ 比较级与最高级： "Sue’s taller than Yoko." / "Sue’s the tallest of the three."。
第3单元：Helping Others (帮助他人与筹款)
• 筹款活动 (Fundraising)： donate (捐赠), have an art fair (办艺术市集), have a cake sale (办蛋糕义卖), have a concert (办音乐会), raise money (筹款), sell tickets (卖票)。
• 宣传方式 (Advertising)： blog (写博客), design posters (设计海报), make a video (做视频), post an article (发布文章)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 提建议 (Could)： "How could we raise money?" / "We could make something and sell it."。
    ◦ 未来计划 (Going to)： "Are you going to have a cake sale?" / "She’s going to write an article."。
第4单元：Shopping Around (购物与比较)
• 购物清单： balloons (气球), beaded bracelet (串珠手链), bouquet of roses (玫瑰花束), gadgets (小玩意/小器具), headphones (耳机), picture frame (相框), silver earrings (银耳饰)。
• 商店地点： card shop (卡片店), craft fair (手工市集), department store (百货公司), flower shop (花店), jewelry store (珠宝店), mall (购物中心)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 多音节词比较： "more/less expensive than", "the most/least expensive"。
    ◦ 同级比较： "not as expensive as"。
    ◦ 程度表达： "The T-shirt is too big. It isn’t small enough."。
第5单元：Vacation Time (假期活动与安全)
• 假期活动： biking (骑行), camping (野营), hiking (远足), horseback riding (骑马), kayaking (划皮划艇), rafting (漂流), skiing (滑雪)。
• 携带物品： helmet (头盔), insect repellent (驱虫剂), life jacket (救生衣), sunscreen (防晒霜), tent (帐篷), water bottle (水瓶), windbreaker (风衣)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 过去进行时： "What was he doing when he got hurt?" / "He was horseback riding when he got hurt."。
    ◦ 特定场景： "What happened while they were hiking?" / "They got lost while they were hiking."。
第6单元：The Future (未来的技术)
• 科技与预测： keyboard jeans (键盘牛仔裤), smartphone (智能手机), video messaging (视频信息), robot (机器人), inventions (发明)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 预测： 使用 will 和 won't 预测未来（"No one will use phone calls."）。
    ◦ 不定代词： 使用 everyone, no one 等谈论未来。
第7单元：What’s It Worth? (事物的价值与用途)
• 核心词汇： abacus (算盘), instant cameras (拍立得), pans (平底锅), sundial (日晷), antiques (古董)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 表达用途： "used for + V-ing" (It’s used for listening to music.) 或 "used to + 动词原形" (It was used to listen to music.)。
    ◦ 推测： 使用 may 或 might 做出假设（"It might be a mirror."）。
第8单元：Where Do They Come From? (来源与材质)
• 材质词汇： clay (黏土), cotton (棉花), metal (金属), rubber (橡胶), wool (羊毛)。
• 产品词汇： blankets (毯子), cola cans (可乐罐), flower pots (花盆), tires (轮胎), sweaters (毛衣)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 被动语态： "Tomatoes are grown in Spain." / "The first chocolate bar was made in South America."。
第9单元：How Adventurous Are You? (探险与味觉描述)
• 描述性形容词： delicious (美味的), raw (生的), sour (酸的), spicy (辣的), sweet (甜的), tasty (可口的), traditional (传统的), unusual (不寻常的)。
• 重点句型：
    ◦ 人生经历： 使用现在完成时搭配 ever（"Have you ever tried sushi?"）。
    ◦ 偏好选择： 使用 would rather（"Would they rather eat or play?" / "They’d rather eat."）。
