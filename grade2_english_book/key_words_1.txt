二年级上册：全单元数据清单
Starter Unit: Our friends & Colours & Numbers
• 单词： red (红), black (黑), yellow (黄), white (白), blue (蓝), green (绿)。
• 数字： one 到 ten (1-10)。
Unit 1 My morning (我的早晨)
• 单词/短语： get up (起床), brush my teeth (刷牙), wash my face (洗脸), get dressed (穿衣服), have breakfast (吃早餐)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “Good morning, Mum!” (早上好，妈妈！)
    ◦ “Brush your teeth.” (刷牙。)
    ◦ “Hurry up and get dressed.” (快点穿好衣服。)
    ◦ “I’m ready for school, Dad!” (爸爸，我准备好去上学了！)
    ◦ “Well done!” (干得好！)
Unit 2 My room (我的房间)
• 方位短语： on the desk (在桌上), in the box (在盒里), under the bed (在床下)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “Where’s my ball?” (我的球在哪？)
    ◦ “What a mess!” (真乱啊！)
    ◦ “Put the books on the shelf.” (把书放在书架上。)
    ◦ “Please put it under the bed.” (请把它放在床底下。)
    ◦ “Put the pencil in the pencil case.” (把铅笔放进铅笔盒里。)
Unit 3 On the way (在路上)
• 短语： by car (乘小汽车), on foot (步行), by bus (乘公共汽车)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “How do you go?” (你怎么去？)
    ◦ “I go to school by bus.” (我乘公交车去上学。)
    ◦ “I love this red bus.” (我喜欢这辆红色公交车。)
    ◦ “It’s the London Underground.” (这是伦敦地铁。)
    ◦ “You use ‘metro’ in Shanghai.” (在上海你们用‘metro’这个词。)
Unit 4 Playing sports (体育运动)
• 单词/短语： run (跑), jump (跳), play ping-pong (打乒乓球), play basketball (打篮球), play football (踢足球)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “You can play ping-pong very well.” (你乒乓球打得很好。)
    ◦ “Can I have a go?” (我能试一下吗？)
    ◦ “You should do warm-ups.” (你应该做准备活动。)
    ◦ “We should wear sports shoes.” (我们应该穿运动鞋。)
    ◦ “Try again!” (再试一次！)
Unit 5 In the sky (在天空中)
• 单词： in the sky (在天空中), star (星), sun (太阳), cloud (云), moon (月亮)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “The sun comes up.” (太阳升起。)
    ◦ “The sun is behind the cloud.” (太阳在云朵后面。)
    ◦ “He goes down at night.” (他在晚上落下。)
    ◦ “The moon and the stars come out.” (月亮和星星出来了。)
Unit 6 In the sea (在海洋里)
• 单词： in the sea (在海里), seahorse (海马), dolphin (海豚), clownfish (小丑鱼)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “I like moving up and down.” (我喜欢上下移动。)
    ◦ “I like swimming far away.” (我喜欢游向远方。)
    ◦ “Let’s play together.” (我们一起玩吧。)
    ◦ “I like playing hide-and-seek in the sea.” (我喜欢在海里玩捉迷藏。)
Unit 7 Seasons (季节)
• 单词： spring (春天), summer (夏天), autumn (秋天), winter (冬天)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “I love to see pretty flowers in spring.” (我喜欢看春天的美丽花朵。)
    ◦ “I love to rest under big trees in summer.” (我喜欢在夏天的大树下休息。)
    ◦ “I love to eat tasty fruit in autumn.” (我喜欢在秋天吃美味的水果。)
    ◦ “I love to play with the white snow in winter.” (我喜欢在冬天玩白雪。)
Unit 8 Yummy fruit (美味的水果)
• 单词： fruit (水果), apple (苹果), pear (梨), banana (香蕉), grape (葡萄), orange (橙子)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “What do you need?” (你需要什么？)
    ◦ “Two grapes, please. Here you are.” (请给我两个葡萄。给你。)
    ◦ “What else?” (还需要别的吗？)
    ◦ “I love apples. They’re red and sweet.” (我喜欢苹果，它们又红又甜。)
Unit 9 The five senses (五种感官)
• 单词： smell (闻), taste (尝), touch (摸), see (看), hear (听)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “I hear raindrops.” (我听见雨滴声。)
    ◦ “I smell grass in the rain.” (我闻到了雨中的草香。)
    ◦ “What a wonderful world!” (多么美好的世界！)
Unit 10 This is me (这就是我)
• 短语： draw a picture (画画), read a book (看书), make breakfast (做早餐)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “What makes you special?” (什么让你与众不同？)
    ◦ “I can do kung fu!” (我会练功夫！)
    ◦ “I can take care of my dog!” (我会照顾我的狗！)
    ◦ “Everyone is special in their own way.” (每个人都有自己的特别之处。)
