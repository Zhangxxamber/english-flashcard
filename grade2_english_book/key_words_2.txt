二年级下册：全单元数据清单
Unit 1 Holidays (假期)
• 短语： feed the cows (喂奶牛), see a film (看电影), visit Grandma and Grandpa (看望爷爷奶奶), have a good time (过得愉快)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “What do you often do in the holidays?” (假期里你经常做什么？)
    ◦ “I often see a new film.” (我经常看一部新电影。)
    ◦ “I often skate with my dad.” (我经常和爸爸一起滑冰。)
Unit 2 Housework (家务)
• 单词/短语： do the housework (做家务), tidy my room (整理房间), make the bed (铺床), dish (碟；盘)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “Let’s do the housework.” (我们做家务吧。)
    ◦ “I can clean the table.” (我会擦桌子。)
    ◦ “I take the dishes to the kitchen.” (我把盘子拿到厨房。)
    ◦ “Come and wash the dishes.” (过来洗碗。)
    ◦ “Let’s wash the dishes and dry them.” (我们来洗碗并把它们擦干。)
    ◦ “You have a white nose.” (你有个白鼻子。)
Unit 3 Feelings (感受)
• 单词： happy (高兴的), excited (激动的), upset (难过的), angry (愤怒的)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “We’re the winners!” (我们是赢家！)
    ◦ “I’m so excited.” (我太激动了。)
    ◦ “Are you OK? You look upset.” (你还好吗？你看起来很难过。)
    ◦ “I can’t find my book.” (我找不到我的书了。)
    ◦ “Let me help you.” (让我来帮你。)
    ◦ “Hi! How do you feel today?” (嗨！你今天感觉怎么样？)
Unit 4 I like robots (我喜欢机器人)
• 短语： play music (放音乐), sing and dance (唱歌跳舞), talk with the baby (和婴儿说话)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “Hello, my name’s Botty. Can I help you?” (你好，我的名字叫Botty。我能帮你吗？)
    ◦ “Wow! You’re so clever.” (哇！你真聪明。)
    ◦ “What can Botty do?” (Botty会做什么？)
    ◦ “He can sing. He’s great.” (他会唱歌。他很棒。)
    ◦ “Can you stand on one leg?” (你能单脚站立吗？)
Unit 5 Friends (朋友)
• 短语： play a game (玩游戏), talk and share (交谈和分享), have a go (尝试)。
• 核心对话 (狮子与老鼠的故事)：
    ◦ “Got you, Little Mouse!” (抓住你了，小老鼠！)
    ◦ “Let me go. Someday I can help you!” (放我走吧。总有一天我会帮你的！)
    ◦ “You help me? Ha-ha-ha!” (你帮我？哈哈哈！)
    ◦ “OK, you can go now.” (好吧，你现在可以走了。)
Unit 6 Mothers (母亲)
• 单词： nice (令人愉快的), cook (厨师), great (伟大的), teacher (教师)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “It’s so quiet today. Where is everyone?” (今天真安静。大家都在哪儿？)
    ◦ “Where are you, Rita?” (你在哪儿，Rita？)
    ◦ “I’m in the kitchen.” (我在厨房里。)
    ◦ “I’m in my bedroom.” (我在我的卧室里。)
Unit 7 Plants (植物)
• 单词： tree (树), leaf (叶子，复数为 leaves), flower (花), grass (草)。
• 核心对话/句型：
    ◦ “Look at the tree in summer. The leaves are green.” (看夏天的树，叶子是绿色的。)
Unit 8 Near the water (在水边)
• 单词： duck (鸭), pond (池塘), frog (青蛙), lake (湖), river (河), shark (鲨鱼), sea (海)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “Are you twins? Your names sound alike.” (你们是双胞胎吗？你们的名字听起来很像。)
    ◦ “‘Jiang’ means ‘river’ and ‘Pu’ means ‘riverside’.” (“江”代表河流，“浦”代表河边。)
Unit 9 Wash my hands (洗手)
• 单词/短语： wash my hands (洗手), before eating (吃东西前), after using the toilet (上厕所后), dirty (脏的), clean (干净的)。
• 核心对话/故事句型：
    ◦ “I like dirty hands.” (我喜欢脏手。)
    ◦ “Wash your hands before eating, dear.” (亲爱的，吃东西前洗手。)
    ◦ “You should keep your hands clean.” (你应该保持手部清洁。)
Unit 10 Good night (晚安)
• 单词/短语： drink some milk (喝牛奶), say good night (道晚安), sleep (睡觉), read a story (读故事)。
• 核心对话：
    ◦ “It’s time for bed.” (该睡觉了。)
    ◦ “Can you read a story to me?” (你能给我读个故事吗？)
    ◦ “Put away your toys first.” (先收好你的玩具。)
    ◦ “Is your schoolbag ready for tomorrow?” (你明天的书包准备好了吗？)
